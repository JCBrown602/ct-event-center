About King Renny's Festival

King Renny's Festival & Faire was founded with the vision of bringing history, legend, and fantasy to life. From jousting tournaments to fire-breathers, we offer an unforgettable experience for all ages. Whether you're seeking adventure, shopping, or just a good turkey leg, our faire welcomes you with open arms and spirited cheers!
