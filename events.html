Festival Events

Adventure, entertainment, and wonder await at every corner of King Renny's Festival!

Performances

Barely Balanced

High-energy acrobatics and comedy that will leave you laughing and gasping in awe!

The Raven

Prepare for a mystical show filled with sleight-of-hand, magic, and juggling tricks beyond belief.

The Washing Well Wenches

Prepare to laugh until you cry as the Washing Well Wenches serve up good clean fun (and plenty of mischief) in their hilarious interactive comedy show!

The Royal Falconer

Marvel at the majesty of falcons, eagles, and owls as the Royal Falconer presents breathtaking aerial displays and tales of ancient falconry traditions.

Live Chess Match

Witness a thrilling live-action chess match as the Royal Guard and the Militia, led by Mongo the Mongrel, battle for victory — every move decided by sword and skill!

Challenges & Games

Archery Booth

Test your skill with bow and arrow and prove your mettle as a true marksman!

Axe Throwing

Channel your inner warrior and hurl axes at distant targets — a true crowd favorite!

Tower Climb

Scale the towering castle wall! Brave knights and daring adventurers of all ages can test their strength and courage on the Tower Climb.

Royal Court

Meet Queen Isabella!

Greet Her Majesty Queen Isabella during royal gatherings throughout the day! Adorned in regal splendor, Queen Isabella brings wisdom, warmth, and the true spirit of festival joy to all her guests.

Princess Sofia and Princess Catalina

Our young royals, Princess Sofia and Princess Catalina, bring joy and wonder to the faire. Seek them out for stories, smiles, and royal greetings!

Queen's Court

Meet the heart of the Kingdom! Queen Isabella and her royal daughters, Princess Sofia and Princess Catalina, welcome all guests with grace and festive cheer.

Artisans & Attractions

Artisan Village

Wander through our Artisan Village and discover handcrafted treasures from master craftsmen.

Live Music

Stroll the festival grounds to the sounds of lutes, drums, and merry voices! Enjoy live performances from traveling minstrels, lively bands, and courtly musicians throughout the day.

Live Mermaids

Marvel at the magic of mermaids who sing, swim, and enchant from their watery realm.

Kids & Family Fun

Primrose the Bubble Fairy

Delightful bubble magic, storytelling, and interactive fun for the young and young-at-heart!

Face Painting

Transform into a knight, dragon, fairy, or royal with dazzling face paint designs.

Plenty of Fun!

From whimsical fairy encounters to daring climbing adventures, King Renny’s Festival offers a realm of wonder for our youngest adventurers! Magic, laughter, and excitement await children of all ages.
